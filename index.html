About

I'm a Data & AI Evangelist, keynote speaker, open source advocate, and professor. With over 15 years of experience driving enterprise-scale analytics transformation for Fortune 100 companies, I've built developer relations, education, and evangelism programs at Fivetran, Tobiko Data, and Dremio.

My work focuses on making complex data and AI concepts accessible to practitioners everywhere. As co-author of Apache Polaris: The Definitive Guide and a graduate professor of data science, I bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical application.

I combine multiple master's degrees in data analytics and business management with hands-on leadership experience to help organizations and individuals unlock the power of modern data architectures.
